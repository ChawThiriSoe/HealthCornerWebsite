About Health Corner

Health Corner is an online health care information sharing website. Our mission is to present high quality, relevant health and wellness information that is trusted and easy to understand anytime anywhere on any device – for free. Our aim is to provide the people with accurate and relevant medical information to help their own health and the health of the people they care. We hope to improve the public health on online through our website.



What you’ll find on Health Corner

We provide information about
1. Diseases and conditions
2. Treatments
3. Symptoms
4. Other medical knowledges
5. Self-help guides for common illnesses and diseases



Our Service

Users can learn medical knowledges in our website. They can also share their knowledges. Patients can share their health status and can ask tips on how to treat for their condition or others. They can also search the specific specialist doctors for a particular disease. Doctors can share the medical knowledges and information about diseases. They can also answer to the patients for tips on how to treat through patients’ posts.
